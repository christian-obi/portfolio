About Me

Blockchain Data Analyst &
Speaker

I am a data scientist and blockchain analyst specializing in turning complex on-chain data into clear, actionable intelligence. I build interactive dashboards, statistical models, and AI-driven tools that decode DeFi protocols, track whale behavior, and surface insights across ecosystems like Core, NEAR, Arbitrum, and Monad.

As a speaker, I share insights on blockchain analytics and data-driven decision making at conferences and industry events. Whether it's visualizing onchain metrics or speaking on stage, I bring a detail-oriented and innovative approach to every project.
